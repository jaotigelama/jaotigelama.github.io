Internship Adentis

JavaScript, HTML, CSS, SQL, C#

Introduction to the internship

This internship was carried out within the scope of the Applied Project Curricular Unit of the Degree in Computer Science.
During the internship, the host entity was Adentis, a technological consulting company focused on innovation and specialized in the development of projects in information systems. It is located at Avenida Barbosa du Bocage 113 - 1st Left in Lisbon.
256 hours of internship were carried out over 32 days.
The regime was mixed, mostly teleworking. Part of a team of 3 elements as a full stack developer.




The Main Project

The web project consists of a system to help the operational management of a client. The operational management involved the management of customer information, services provided, processes in progress and allowed the consultation of important metrics for the analysis of business performance. In this project I followed methodologies and standards such as the waterfall model, onion architecture, REST APIs and dependency injection, I worked both front-end using React and back-end using .Net Core.

Technologies: JavaScript, HTML, CSS, SQL, C#

Tools: SQL Server, SQL Server Management Studio, Visual Studio Code, Visual Studio 2019, Git, Microsoft Teams

Others: REST API, Dependency injection, Waterfall, Onion architecture, Entity Framework, Auto Mapper, Bootstrap, React, .Net Core
